BrainWorks - The Brain Training Game!!

About the app


Did you know that playing Brain-training games can decrease your risk of obtaining dementia in later years?

We have a wide array of games targeting the different areas of the brain; Memory, Speed, Attention and Problem-Solving.

Choose games from different areas to get the most out of the training!

You can visit this website to see how you fare against other people in the area and check your high scores daily!

It is highly important that you work on your brain continuously so brush the cobwebs away and get training!
